### Section-by-Section Image Guidance
**Hero Section**
- Full-width background image (1920x1080 minimum concept)
- Add dark gradient overlay: `bg-gradient-to-r from-black/70 via-black/50 to-transparent`
- Or use: `bg-gradient-to-b from-transparent to-black/60` for bottom text

**About/Team Section**
- Team photos: circular crops with `rounded-full`
- Or: Side-by-side layout with large image + text
- Consider: Image on left, content on right (or vice versa)

**Testimonials**
- Avatar images: Small circular headshots
- Background: Subtle gradient or brand color at 10% opacity
- Consider quote styling with large quote marks

**CTA Section**
- Option A: Bold gradient background (no image)
- Option B: Background image with heavy overlay
- Make the button stand out as the focal point

**Footer**
- Dark background (gray-900 or black)
- Simple, no imagery needed
- Consider subtle top border or gradient fade-in

### Background Techniques to Use
- **Hero**: Full-bleed background image with dark overlay (bg-black/50) for text readability
- **Feature sections**: Subtle gradients or light textured backgrounds
- **Testimonials**: Soft gradient or branded color background
- **CTA sections**: Bold gradient or contrasting background color
- **Cards**: Consider subtle shadows and hover effects
