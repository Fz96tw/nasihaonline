The Nasiha Contribution Hours System

Every hour you contribute — teaching, mentoring, curating — earns you one Nasiha Contribution Hour. Contribution Hours can be spent to access expert consultations and mentorship in return.

Critically: Contribution Hours are recognition, not gatekeeping. All members retain full access to the community regardless of their balance.

About Nasiha

Nasiha — meaning sincere advice and guidance — was founded on the belief that knowledge is a shared resource, not a commodity.

Our Mission
"Nasiha is dedicated to spreading knowledge by encouraging each one of us to learn and to teach. We exist to create a community of learners focused on knowledge sharing — one that acknowledges the willingness of those around us who help us learn, and that shares knowledge unconditionally with others."
Core Values
Knowledge as a Common Good — Medical expertise should be shared, not hoarded. No exchange within Nasiha carries a fee.
Reciprocity — Those who receive guidance contribute in kind, sustaining a cycle of giving.
Inclusion & Accessibility — Open to members from all corners of the world, all career stages, all disciplines.
Trust & Integrity — Honest, evidence-based exchange. Knowledge shared responsibly.
Humility & Lifelong Learning — No one is only a teacher. Every expert is also a student.
Our Vision

A world in which every medical professional — regardless of where they practice, what they can afford, or where they are in their career — has access to a trusted network of peers, mentors, and experts committed to the advancement of their craft and the wellbeing of patients everywhere.

How to Join Nasiha

Membership is open internationally to anyone engaged in medicine, healthcare, or public health who shares our values.

Admission Phases
1
Founding Cohort — Invite Only
Personally selected by founding members to establish community culture and standards.
2
Referral-Driven Growth
Existing members refer prospective applicants. Referral serves as a community voucher. Current Phase
3
Open Applications
Applications accepted openly once the community is established. Coming soon.

Eligibility
Open to practicing physicians, residents, students, allied health professionals, retired medical professionals, and public health workers who share our values of open knowledge exchange.
