The Model
How Nasiha Works
A pay-it-forward model where every act of teaching enables an act of learning.
Research
Members find and curate resources — articles, guidelines, readings — and share them with the community to deepen collective expertise.
Review
Members guide each other to improve through honest, constructive feedback rooted in a shared commitment to growth.
Teach
Members share knowledge through lectures and case discussions — because knowledge is a common good.
Our Currency
The NASIHA Knowledge Exchange System

Every hour you contribute — teaching, curating — earns you one Knowledge Hour. Hours can be spent to access expert knowledge in return.

Critically: Knowledge Hours are recognition, not gatekeeping. All members retain full access to the community regardless of their balance.

About Nasiha

Nasiha — meaning sincere advice and guidance — was founded on the belief that knowledge is a shared resource, not a commodity.

Our Mission
Why We Exist
"NASIHA is dedicated to spreading knowledge by encouraging each one of us to learn and to teach. We exist to create a community of learners focused on knowledge sharing — one that acknowledges the willingness of those around us who help us learn, and that shares knowledge freely with others."
Values
Core Principles
Knowledge as a Common Good — Knowledge should be shared, not hoarded. No exchange within NASIHA carries a fee.
Reciprocity — Those who receive guidance contribute in kind, sustaining a cycle of giving.
Inclusion & Accessibility — Open to members from all corners of the world, all career stages, all disciplines.
Trust & Integrity — Honest, evidence-based exchange. Knowledge shared responsibly.
Humility & Lifelong Learning — No one is only a teacher. Every expert is also a student.
Vision
Our Vision

A world in which every person — regardless of where they live, what they can afford, or where they are in their path to learning — has access to a trusted network of peers, advisors, and experts committed to the advancement of their craft and sharing their knowledge.

Dedicated To

NASIHA — Dedicated to Narjis and Syed Iftikhar Hussain Abidi, who guided us toward a life of learning.

Privacy Policy

How Nasiha collects, uses, and protects member information.

What We Collect
Information You Provide

Name, email, professional title, specialty, career stage, country/region, bio, areas of expertise, and — if you choose to upload one — a profile photo. Membership applications also collect a professional reference once required by the current admission phase.

How We Use It
Running Nasiha, Nothing Else

Member data is collected only to operate the community — membership review, the Member Directory, the Knowledge Hours ledger, event RSVPs, and community communications. Nasiha never sells, shares, or uses member data for any purpose outside of running the Organization.

Your Controls
Directory Visibility & Data Rights

You control whether you appear in the Member Directory and what's visible there. You may request an export or deletion of your personal data at any time from Settings. Historical Knowledge Hours ledger entries and content attribution may be retained in de-identified form to preserve the integrity of the community's audit trail.

Patient Information
Never Shared, Anywhere

Identifiable patient information must never appear in Nasiha — not in case discussions, library submissions, forum posts, or messages. Case-based content requires explicit confirmation that all information has been de-identified before it can be shared.

Questions or Requests

To request your data, ask a question, or report a concern, contact us at contact@nasihaonline.com or visit the Contact page.
